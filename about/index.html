ABOUT

The goal of astrotium is to provide the latest news in space from all parts of our universe. I created this website in frustration in how space news was being written. Depending on the topic, I often felt underwhelmed and not motivated to continue reading. However, with astrotium, I aim to tell each news article as a story. Each story gives a problem and you read the author work out and try to solve the challenge, gradually gaining new information. I aim to give my thoughts and speculation as well as end with open questions and my own speculation and opinions.

If you want to write an article on this site showing your own theories or speculation on space science, then I take requests here.
