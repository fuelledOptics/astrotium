CONTRIBUTE

Do you have an opinion on space, the universe or human exploration you want to share?

Here on astrotium, I allow you to write your own guest article expressing your opinions or ideas and if it is to a high standard, then it will be published on the website. You will be credited in the article and have the chance to express your ideas or opinions.


Guidelines for an article:

- You must supply the article content inline in the email. ie. the article must NOT be written in a separate file and attached to the email.

- Have original content - do not copy other people's work.

- You must have good English grammar and spelling. Make sure to proofread the article as we may not accept articles which are badly written.

- Make it relavent - it must be somewhat related to space, the universe, human exploration, space science, physics.

- Your article may be edited for any reason.

- Your article may not be accepted for any reason.

- Keep the article short - between 500 - 2000 words.

- You are allowed 1 relavent image. The image may be rejected for any reason.

- You need to supply a name with your article. - If you feel inclined not to, you do not have to provide a real name. Just one which sounds authentic.


Articles may take from a few days to a few months to publish. I may not contact you when your article goes online.
